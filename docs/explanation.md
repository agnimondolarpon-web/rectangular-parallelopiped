# Rectangular Parallelopiped — Formula Explanation

## What is a Rectangular Parallelopiped?

A **Rectangular Parallelopiped** (also called a **Cuboid**) is a 3D solid shape with:
- 6 rectangular faces
- 12 edges
- 8 vertices

All angles are right angles (90°).

---

## Variables

| Variable | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| `l` | Length |
| `w` | Width  |
| `h` | Height |

---

## Surface Area

The total surface area is the sum of all 6 faces:

```
Area = 2 × ((l × w) + (w × h) + (h × l))
```

**Why?**
- Top + Bottom face = `2 × (l × w)`
- Front + Back face = `2 × (w × h)`
- Left + Right face = `2 × (h × l)`

---

## Volume

Volume is the space enclosed inside:

```
Volume = l × w × h
```

---

## Example

Given: `l = 5`, `w = 4`, `h = 3`

```
Area   = 2 × ((5×4) + (4×3) + (3×5))
       = 2 × (20 + 12 + 15)
       = 2 × 47
       = 94 square units

Volume = 5 × 4 × 3
       = 60 cubic units
```
